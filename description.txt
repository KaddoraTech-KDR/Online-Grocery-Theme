Description: Kaddora is a clean, modern, and highly flexible WooCommerce WordPress theme designed specifically for grocery stores, supermarkets, organic food shops, hypermarkets, and online retailers selling fresh produce or household goods. Built with the latest WordPress block editor technology, Kaddora delivers fast performance, pixel-perfect layouts, and seamless shopping experiences on any device.
Packed with pre-built templates and one-click demo import, Kaddora allows you to launch a professional-looking grocery or supermarket website in minutes. Its intuitive drag-and-drop interface and Gutenberg/Block Editor support make it easy for anyone to customize colors, fonts, page layouts, and content without writing code.
Kaddora integrates deeply with WooCommerce, giving you all the tools you need for a full-featured online store: product catalog, advanced filtering, AJAX cart, wishlists, product quick view, customer accounts, and secure checkout. The theme is fully responsive, SEO-optimized, translation-ready, and built to handle high volumes of products and traffic.
